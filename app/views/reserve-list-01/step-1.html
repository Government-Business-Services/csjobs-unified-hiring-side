What is a reserve list?

Candidates who passed the recruitment process are placed on the reserve list for 1 year when there isn't a role available for them.

You could save time and resources by recruiting from the reserve list.

To recruit from the reserve list you will:

  • look through their previous successful application
  • arrange a phone call to see if they are suitable for the role
