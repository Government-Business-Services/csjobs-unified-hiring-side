Departmental questions

Your department requires all candidates to answer the following questions before starting their application as a matter of policy:

  • Candidates that accept an interview invite will need to take and pass a compulsory drug test. If appointed, the role holder will be subject to random drug testing throughout their first year of employment.

    Are you able to comply with this policy?

  • This role requires the highest security clearance known as Developed Vetting (or DV). Candidates should ensure they are familiar and comfortable with the vetting standard before they starting their application.

    Are you able to comply with this policy?

    • What does Developed Vetting mean?
    Developed Vetting requires candidates to be interviewed by our vetting specialists, face-to-face and asked to discuss personal topics such as their drug use, political interests, financial affairs, their wider family background and relationships.
