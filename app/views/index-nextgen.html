(1) Vacancy is set up
Event Who Status Testing notes
Vacancy holder starts the process of creating a new vacancy
Notes
They provide a few simple details at the start. The proposed job title and the grade, the profession that the role will be aligned to and finally their department. This information is assumed to be readily available to vacancy holders, as it is required during the business case and approval step.

The approval process is department-specific , but this common basic information should be attainable.

We use this information throughout the service, and certain features are dependent on them.
