Your health declaration

Why we ask about your health

We ask about your health because:

  • if you have a disability or health condition we need to understand how it could affect your ability to carry out the tasks of the job you're applying for
  • it enables us to consider adjustments we could make to help you take up employment with us and carry out the duties of the job effectively
  • we must be confident that your attendance record will be good

Information we will need

We need to know about:

  • any adjustments you think you might need
  • any health conditions that could affect your work
  • any absences in the last 2 years that lasted more than 16 days
  • any health issues you might want to discuss with an occupational health provider
You can save your progress at any time and come back later to give us the rest of the information.
